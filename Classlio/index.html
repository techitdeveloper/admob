Privacy Policy – Classlio

Effective Date: 2026-01-19

Classlio is an offline attendance tracking application provided as an ad-supported service. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information Collection and Use

User-Entered Data

All attendance data, class details, student names, and records entered into Classlio are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or have access to this data.

Third-Party Advertising

Classlio uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect limited information such as Advertising ID and IP address in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Consumer Privacy Rights

Users may delete all app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app. Personalized ads can be managed via Android device settings.

3. Children’s Privacy

Classlio is not directed to children and is intended for educators and professionals. Student names entered in the app remain stored locally and are not collected by the developer.

4. Data Retention

No cloud storage is used. All data is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

5. Security

Data is stored using Android’s internal storage protections.
